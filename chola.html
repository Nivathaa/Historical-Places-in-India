THE GREAT CHOLA TEMPLE

TAMILNADU

The Great Living Chola Temples in Tamil Nadu are a group of UNESCO World Heritage Sites and the epitome of artistic and architectural excellence. They are one of the top historical places in India that were built by the kings of the Chola Empire, a powerful empire that stretched all over South India and the neighboring islands. The three great temples are Brihadeshwara Temple at Tanjore, Airavateshwara Temple in Darasuram, and Brihadeshwara Temple in Gangaikonda Cholapuram.

Every one of these temples reflects the outstanding achievements of the Chola Empire in sculpture, architecture, painting, and bronze casting.
